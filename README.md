# Knowledge Gap Formulation for Compounding

---

## Overview

This repository is my final submission for **Knowledge Gap Formulation for Compounding**. It documents a paired research sprint where I turned unresolved engineering questions from my portfolio into sharpened questions, public explainers, social threads, partner signoffs, and grounding commits. Across the four-day scope, I worked through seven unique questions total: four gaps I named from my own work and three peer gaps I researched, with Day 1 being a self-researched gap. The artifacts connect back to **The Conversion Engine**, **Tenacious-Bench and the DPO Judge**, and **Document Intelligence Refinery**, showing how each research cycle improved an existing system rather than remaining purely theoretical.
